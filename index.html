Seed Cultivation Guidelines

Standard Seed Sowing Isolation Checklist

Critical instructions to maintain genetic purity and pass inspection.

  • Maintain a minimum isolation distance of 3 meters from other wheat varieties to prevent pollen cross-contamination.
  • Use only approved Foundation/Breeder source seed tag tags provided at registration. Save physical tags.
  • Ensure mechanical rogueing: uproot and remove off-type plants and visual weeds before the flowering stage.
  • Keep soil moisture adequate but avoid waterlogging during germination and flowering stages.
